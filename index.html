What is?

The Coronavirus (2019-nCoV) is a virus identified as the cause of an outbreak of respiratory illness first detected in Wuhan, China (December 2019). Early on, many of the patients reportedly had some link to a large animal market. However, the last patients have not had exposure to animal markets, suggesting person-to-person spread is occurring. Transmission

Human Touch

Touching or shaking hands with a person infected can pass the virus from one person to another.

Cough and Sneeze

Coughing and sneezing without covering the mouth can spreading the virus. We recommend use mouth mask.

Unclean hands

Making contact with infected objects and then touching your nose, eyes, or mouth. Allways wash your hands.

Symptoms

Patients have mild to severe respiratory illness with other types of symptoms like:

- Fever

- Cough

- Shortness of breath

Prevention & Treatment

Currently no vaccine to prevent 2019-nCoV infection. But the best way to prevent 2019-nCoV is to avoid being exposed to this virus. Right now is spreading to another countries and our goverments are doing all thee best work to prevent this possible pandemia.

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention recommend:

- Wash you hand with soap for at least 20 seconds

- Avoid conntact with people who are sick

- Desinfect frecuently touched object
